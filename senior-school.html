WELCOME TO THE SECONDARY SCHOOL

The school runs the Nigerian and British curriculum. The academic curriculum of the college is divided into two segments; namely; the Junior Secondary school and the Senior Secondary School in strict compliance with the National Policy on Education. In the first three years, all students follow a common curriculum which provides widely-based courses centered on the acquisition of study skills and fundamentals which prepares them for the demand of the third form examination. The subjects offered are English language, Mathematics, Business Studies, Basic Science, Basic technology, Home Economics, Art, French Computer Studies, Religious Studies, Music, and Agricultural Science.

EXAMINATIONS OFFERED

The College is a government approved international private school. It is accredited by the Lagos State Government and the Federal Government of the Republic of Nigeria to instruct and train students for the following examinations:
1. Junior Secondary School examination (BECE, checkpoint)
2. Senior Secondary School Certificate Examination (WAEC, NECO)

The school also prepare students for the International General certificate of Secondary Education and the Cambridge A level examination. In addition, the school is an approved Centre for these examinations and a member of the International Schools Association (ISA).

USE OF TECHNOLOGY

Given the fact that the 21st century is witnessing a rapid explosion in Information and Communication Technology (ICT), the college management in its wisdom has put in place a well-equipped internet ready computer laboratory, fully stocked with assorted computers and accessories with trained staff in its employment.

Our computing facilities allow students to use educational and industry-standard software with the intent and purpose of being connected to satellite links on the internet.

In addition, all our classrooms are fully equipped with interactive board and projectors to bring learning to life for the students.

Classroom management software such as NETSUPPORT, MYTHWARE enable the teachers to interact with the students through their laptops.
