About Us

At CALEB INTERNATIONAL COLLEGE attention is aimed at giving all students sound academic training cum faultless moral polishing. This is based on the learning theory in psychology which believes that we live out what we learn and that there is hardly anything we do without learning it. In a nut-shell, our aim is to turn out morally and academically sound products.

Armed, with a vision and inspiration derived from the enduring works of great Christian missionaries and educationists, like George Muller, David Living Stone and others, the proprietor of CALEB INTERNATIONAL COLLEGE, an educationists, former inspector of Education and a man of God is fully turned to inculcate priceless gifts upon any child that passes through the school. No serious parent can allow his or her son/daughter to pass through a system that will make them thorns in the flesh in the future.

Remember "TRAIN UP YOUR CHILD IN THE WAY HE SHOULD GO". Caleb is the place for that TRAINING

At CALEB INTERNATIONAL COLLEGE we believe that there are certain major elements to be taken into consideration when selecting a school. Obviously of high priority is a sound academic background. We seek to combine that which is best in traditional education with those modern developments which have proved to be desirable and effective.

To this end we provide :

✓ Individual and personal attention

✓ A broad range of subjects taught by qualified and teachers

✓ A strong sixth form which will prepare each student for higher education or direct entry to career training


Equally important is the social atmosphere. Within the Caleb International College Community, students learn how to get on with others, live happily and constructively, the students also respond positively to work hard and sensible discipline. But work is not just academic work, it is the constructive use of time. The physical, cultural and academic development of each student is encouraged through a wide range of school activities. Students are given opportunities to excel in many different areas of sport and the performing arts.
